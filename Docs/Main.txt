== What it does ==
Alt-Click to Add Item will add the ability to alt-click an item in your bag to add it a mail, auction, or trade frame, and alt-click to buy a stack if reagents.

== Where to get it ==
*[http://www.wowace.com/projects/alt-click-to-add-item/files/ WoW Ace] - Often Beta quality
*[http://wow.curse.com/downloads/wow-addons/details/alt-click-to-add-item.aspx Curse] - Most updated stable version
